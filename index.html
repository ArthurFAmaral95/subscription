About London

London is the capital and largest city of England an the United Kingdom, with population of just over 9 million people. Its ancient core and finantial center was founded by the Romans as Londinium and retains boundaries close to its medieval ones.

As one of the world`s major global cities, London exerts a strong influence on its arts, entertainment, fashion, commerce and finance, education, health care, media, science and technology, tourism, transport and comunication

London`s diverse cultures encompass over 300 languages. It is one of the most visited cities in the world and is home to the most 5-star hotels of ant city. Some of the city landmarks include: Buckingham Palace, the London Eye, Piccadilly Circus, St Paul`s Cathedral, Tower Bridge and Trafalgar Square.
